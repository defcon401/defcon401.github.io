Who we are

dc401 is Rhode Island's DEF CON Group — a loose collective of curious people who are also curious. We meet sporadically to share projects, trash talk vendors and generally talk about security. From n00b to 313373 and everyone in between the only requirement is curiosity, all are welcome.

Note: We are not affiliated with Hackers on Planet Earth (HOPE); "Hope" has been Rhode Island's official motto since 1664 so you know...technically they stole that from us.
